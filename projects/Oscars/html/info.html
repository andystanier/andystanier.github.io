Information

The Best of The Oscars is a film-lovers mobile website which helps users identify films that the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has awarded Best Picture for the year in which they were made.

The Oscars®, as the Academy Awards have become known, have been awarded since 1929 for the films made in 1927/28, and the Outstanding Picture or Best Picture award has been given every year since.

This website provides you with a comprehensive list of all the films which have won the top award and enables you to tick them off once you have watched them. You can keep an eye on how many you have seen using the progress bar on the main page.

The films are organised by date and can also be viewed by title. Swipe left or right from a film detail page to view the next or previous year.

Film information in this app is taken from Wikipedia and IMDB.
